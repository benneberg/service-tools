SignageOS – Un Lock StandAloneDevice

Enter the device IP (assigned during Google provisioning), the device policy ID you wish to remove, and optionally Org ID and your support user id for audit.


      

    


    
    

      
Manual Guide

      

        
Manual Procedure Guide

        
Note: Only remove the policy for authorized requests to prevent accidental security risks.

        
    
          
  1. Log into the SignageOS portal (box.signageos) with a Dise account that has admin rights.
    2. 
          
  2. Navigate to Organizations → [Target Org] → Devices.
    3. 
          
  3. Search for the device by ID, serial number, or name.
    4. 
          
  4. Click the device and go to Policies.
    5. 
          
  5. Find the Standalone Mode Locked policy.
    6. 
          
  6. Click Remove / Disable.
    7. 
          
  7. Confirm removal.
    8. 
          
  8. Verify the device no longer has the policy applied.
    9. 
          
  9. Set the standalone mode to unlocked and verify the setting is now persistent by restarting the app via the app settings in the upper-right menu.
    10. 
          
  10. Communicate back to the partner that the device is now unlocked.
    11. 
          
  11. Inform the partner that they need to notify support when the device should be locked again.
    12.
